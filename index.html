TrackinTrade - Your Ultimate Trading Journal

TrackinTrade helps traders store, analyze, and optimize their trades for consistent profitability.

Documentation Source Code

Benefits

Built for traders who care about data security and privacy

You are 100% in control of your data. Download and install TradeNote and the database wherever you want.

Built for individuals that need simplicity and flexibility

TrackinTrade is designed to be a simple yet powerful trading journal, that you can modify your way with an open core codebase.

Features

Import

Import trades from your broker platform.

Visualize

Visualize your trades on charts and get meaningful insight from your data.

Filter

Filter your results by date, position, patterns, mistakes, and much more.

Diary

Keep a daily diary of your trading journey to work on your trader psychology.

Satisfaction

Mark each of your trades and days as good or bad depending on your trading rules.

Screenshots

Add and annotate chart screenshots to reflect on trade entries or setups.

Price Charts

Visualize Price Charts with entry and exit points of your trades.

Excursions

Add MFE prices automatically for deeper efficiency analysis.

Tags

Add tags and tag groups to identify patterns, setups, or mistakes.

Manage Imports

Clean your imports with one simple click.

Responsive

Use TradeNote on your mobile devices.
